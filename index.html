Typing Tips & FAQ

What's the proper typing posture?
Sit up straight, keep your feet flat on the floor, position your wrists slightly above the keyboard, and maintain a comfortable distance from the screen. Your fingers should be curved and relaxed on the home row keys.
How can I improve my typing speed?
Practice daily for 10-15 minutes, focus on accuracy before speed, use all fingers with proper posture, and maintain a consistent rhythm. Start slowly and gradually increase your speed as muscle memory develops.
What's a good typing speed?
The average typing speed is 35-40 WPM. Professional typists usually type 65-75 WPM, while competitive typists can exceed 100 WPM. Focus on accuracy first - 95% accuracy or higher is ideal.
How is WPM calculated?
Words Per Minute (WPM) is calculated by dividing the total characters typed (including spaces) by 5, then dividing by the time in minutes. This gives a standardized measure regardless of word length.
